## Overview

EasyDown is engineered to deliver high-fidelity video and audio extraction without compromising quality. Unlike browser extensions that often fail due to platform updates, EasyDown utilizes a cloud-based parsing engine to ensure high success rates for TikTok, YouTube, Spotify, and other major platforms.

## Core Capabilities

* **Lossless Extraction**: Download videos in original 4K/1080P resolution.
* **Watermark Removal**: Automatically detects and removes platform watermarks (TikTok/Douyin).
* **Audio Separation**: High-bitrate MP3 extraction from video sources.
* **Privacy Focused**: No user registration or installation required.
